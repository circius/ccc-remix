• Click a bookname to resume a work in progress.
  • 'Create new book' does what it says on the tin.
  • The scanner sorts scans as 'left' and 'right.' Make sure the page on the left of the screen is on the left in the book!
  • You can try pressing 'F' to flip the cameras.
  • Scans are in the subfolder 'scans' of wherever this script is.
  • Once you've processed your book you should clean that folder up if you know how to.
  • 'Q' will return you to the menu. (so will F5...)
